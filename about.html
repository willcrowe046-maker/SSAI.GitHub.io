Our Story

Started Because Sydney
Deserved Better

PlumbCo was founded after seeing too many Sydney homeowners get burned by dodgy quotes, no-show tradespeople, and sky-high after-hours surcharges. The local plumbing market had a trust problem — and we started PlumbCo to fix it.

Our model is simple: show up when we say we will, charge a fair price (quoted upfront), and back every job with a lifetime labour warranty. Nothing complicated about it.

Today, PlumbCo employs a team of fully licensed plumbers across Greater Sydney — local people who know their suburbs, understand the plumbing challenges in Sydney's older housing stock, and take pride in leaving every job better than they found it.
