Common Questions

Got Questions?

How much does a plumber cost in Sydney?

PlumbCo charges a $0 call out fee — you pay nothing just for us to show up. Before any work starts, your plumber will give you a fixed-price quote. You approve it, then we work. No hourly rates, no hidden extras.

Are your plumbers licensed in NSW?

Yes. Every PlumbCo plumber holds a current NSW plumbing licence and is fully insured. We're also members of Master Plumbers NSW. Our contractor licence number is #330742C — you can verify this at licence.fairtrading.nsw.gov.au.

How fast can you come for an emergency?

Our target is on-site within 60 minutes for emergencies across Greater Sydney. We operate 24/7 — including weekends and public holidays — with no after-hours surcharge on our call out fee.

Do you offer a warranty on your work?

Yes — every job comes with a lifetime labour warranty. If anything we've worked on fails due to our workmanship, we'll come back and fix it at no cost to you. We also pass on manufacturer warranties on parts and systems we install.
