Service FAQs

Which areas of Sydney do you cover?

We cover all of Greater Sydney including Inner West, Eastern Suburbs, Northern Beaches, North Shore, Western Sydney, Sutherland Shire, Hills District, and the CBD. If you're in Sydney metro, we're coming.

Do you serve commercial properties?

Yes. We work with property managers, strata companies, retail businesses, restaurants, and commercial buildings across Sydney. We provide full job reports and are available for ongoing maintenance contracts.

Are there extra charges for after-hours jobs?

Our $0 call out fee applies 24/7 — no extra charge for after-hours, weekends, or public holidays just to show up. Your plumber will quote the job price on-site, which you approve before we start.

How do I know your plumbers are licensed?

Our NSW Contractor Licence #330742C is publicly verifiable at licence.fairtrading.nsw.gov.au. Every plumber we send carries their licence and will show it on request. We're also members of Master Plumbers NSW.
